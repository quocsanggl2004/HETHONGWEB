Apple Bavarian Torte (★ ★ ★ ★)

A classic Eruopean torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanila
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanila extract
  • 6 apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat owen to 450 degree F (230 degree C)
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, vanila, and flour.
  3. Press crust mixture into the flat bottom of a 9.inch springform pan. Set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl blend cream cheese and sugar. Beat in egg and vanila Pour cheese mixture over crust.
  5. Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon. Spead apple mixture over all.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes Reduce heat to 400 degree F (200 degree C) and continue baking for 25 minutes.
  7. Spinkle almonds over top of torte Continue baking until lighty browed Cool before removing from pan
